Privacy Policy

We Collect No Personal Information in the "Frog and Saw" game

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in the "Frog and Saw" game. Individual settings and gained scores are not personal and are stored only on your device. This data is not shared through the internet.

App Analytics

To help us better understand how people use the game and to continue improving them, we use analytics information provided by App Store Connect. These services provide generalized data about our apps, such as app usage patterns, and other anonymized statistics. This data is completely anonymous and cannot personally identify you in any way.
